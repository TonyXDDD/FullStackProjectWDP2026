Project Name: SafeEats

a. What problem does your app solve or what purpose does it serve?

The purpose of this app is to serve as a central hub for people who struggle to find places to 
eat comfortably due to their food allergies, dietary restrictions, or medical conditions such as 
celiac disease. Ive seen first hand how individuals like my friends or my girlfriend experience a ton of 
stress and uncertainty when dining out because they are unsure whether restaurants can properly accommodate 
their needs, or have safely prepared their food. This website will provide a supportive, community-driven 
platform where users can discover, review, and share information about restaurants that accommodate specific 
dietary requirements.

b. Who is the intended user?

The intended users are people with food allergies, dietary restrictions, or medical conditions 
(such as celiac disease) who need help finding safe places to eat. It is also useful for parents 
or caregivers managing someone’s dietary needs.

c. What actions will users be able to perform (e.g., sign up, add items, search, 
comment, etc.)

My end goal for this project is that users will be able to create an account, log in, and set 
their dietary preferences. They can search for restaurants, filter results based on specific 
allergies or restrictions, post reviews, rate restaurants based on safety and accommodation, 
and comment on other users posts. Users will also be able to edit or delete their own reviews!
